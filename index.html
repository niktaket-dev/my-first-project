About Me

Where

design

Hi, I'm Nikta. My journey started in Mechanical Engineering, where I spent 5 years at Poliran — a leading mechanical building systems company. But my passion for the web never faded.

Two years ago, I took full ownership of the company's website and social media — from WordPress design to content creation and SEO. Now I'm diving deep into HTML & CSS to step fully into the frontend world.

What drives me is the love of learning and the joy of merging art with engineering. Outside of code, I do pottery, play music, and find beauty in that overlap between craft and technology.

My goal: build digital spaces that are both useful and feel genuinely good.
